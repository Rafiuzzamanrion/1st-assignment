Bangladesh floods - urgent appeal

Bangladesh has been hit with the worst flooding in 100 years. Thousands of children are at risk, threatened by a high risk of drowning, disease, and malnutrition. Children and families need emergency supplies

7 year-old Sihab is one of the 1.6 million children affected by the floods that have devasted north-eastern Bangladesh this year. His entire village was hit with severe flooding following the rainfall, leaving his home underwater. His family is sheltering in a school building, without enough food. “I’ve only eaten dried rice with milk today because there are six of us and not enough rations for all of us,” Sihab says.
